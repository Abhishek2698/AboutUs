Our Story

Katkaria Creations has always had its roots deep into creativity and innovation. Beginning our journey in the industry of marble handicrafts manufacturing, our driving force is creating products and services that are out of the box, while keeping in mind their functionality and sustainability. We believe in evolution of our products and services as per the taste and preferences of people, as staying relevant in this fast pacing world is our top priority.

Bring Bar Home

With the eviction of the stigma around the words bar and alcohol and the surge of curiosity in people about bar interiors, making them a crucial part of entertainment and leisure activities, we decided to bring out the concept of Home Bar Interiors and provide a perfect one stop destination where people can find everything that they need to set up a home bar. Starting from Bar Interior Services, Mini Bar Cabinets, Bar Briefcase to Hidden Bar Furniture, Bar Accessories our online and offline store offers everything under one roof.

Creating Sustainable Beauties

Creativity and craftsmanship are the supporting pillars of our organisation, and wood and marble handicrafts top the list of where these two words amalgamate. Our emphasis is on using natural material like wood, natural stones, jute, bamboo, metals etc. instead of artificial material, to bring out the best in the interiors of your homes, as homes epitomize one’s creativity for demonstrating their personal aesthetics.

Our Core Competencies

We have our own in-house manufacturing of wood and marble handicrafts which gives us the freedom to customise and design as per needs of our customers.
Our firm thrives on two core policies Imagination and Innovation. We believe in staying relevant and original.
We have built a team of artisans who have been passed on the art to create marble handicrafts by their ancestors and we take immense pride and responsibility to help them thrive.
We use materials such as jute, drift woods, bamboo, natural stones like marble, agate, soapstone etc. to design and produce home décor articles, lamps, furniture etc.
We offer a wide variety of products and services. Starting from Bar Interior Services, Bar Briefcase, Bar Furniture and Trolleys as well as wide variety of Lamps, Accent Furniture, Wall Décor etc.
Our Bar Furniture and Bar Interiors are achieved by using premium quality engineered wood and is manufactured by the finest woodworkers and carpenters in the country
